About

I'm a final year physics undergraduate student at Universitas Indonesia. I specialize in theoretical nuclear and particle physics, especially in kaon production under the supervision of Prof. Terry Mart. Although I specialize in theoretical nuclear physics, I also studied many-body physics and general relativity. Sometimes, I transcribe what I learned in LaTeX .
