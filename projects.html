  1. Elastic electron-proton scattering (Relatvistic Quantum Mechanics Course, 2023)
    2. I made this project in my fifth semester as an assignment for my relativistic quantum mechanics course. I calculated the differential cross section for elastic electron-proton scattering, known as Rosenbluth's formula. I start with drawing the Feynman diagram for the scattering. From there, I analyzed the diagram using Dirac equation to find the invariant amplitude. After I obtained the amplitude, I calculated the differential cross section. $$\frac{d\sigma}{d\Omega}$$
